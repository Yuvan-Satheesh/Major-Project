Our Mission

The MFHS Teacher Induction System was created to solve the challenges new staff face when joining our school community. We recognized that orientation confusion, inaccessible policy information, and lack of progress tracking were creating unnecessary stress.

Our mission is to provide every new teacher with the tools, resources, and connections they need to transition smoothly into their role, allowing them to focus on what matters most - teaching and connecting with students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can this induction program be used after the completion of the courses?

The formal induction program runs for your first term. However, all resources remain accessible indefinitely.

What technology do I need to access the system?

The system works on any device with internet access. We recommend using Chrome or Edge browsers for optimal performance.

Can I provide feedback about the induction process?

Absolutely! We actively encourage feedback through the system's built-in feedback tool. Your input helps us continuously improve the program.
